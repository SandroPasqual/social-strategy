Belief: Brand strategy built by one voice alone is always incomplete. The client has reality without distance. The strategist has distance without reality. Neither is sufficient alone.

What this changes: Every input field in the Goodspell system has a mirror. The client fills their side — their market, their language, their reality from the inside. The strategist occupies the other — observations, reframes, patterns the client can't see because they're living inside them. The analysis document inverts this. The strategist writes. The client responds.

What this protects: Strategies that look right on paper and feel wrong in practice. Deliverables disconnected from the client's actual reality. Clients who receive a strategy they don't own because they didn't build it.
