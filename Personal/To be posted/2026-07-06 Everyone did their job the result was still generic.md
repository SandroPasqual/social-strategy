Everyone did their job. The result was still generic.

The designer was briefed and delivered. The copywriter wrote to the brief — tight, clean. The strategist handed over a framework. Thorough. Professional.

Each part was correct. Nothing connected.

Not because anyone was incompetent. Because the pieces were made in separate rooms. No one held the thread before anyone else started working.

We've all been in that room. Everyone nodding at the process. The work arrived on time. It just didn't arrive together.
