Everyone did their job. The result was still generic.

The designer was briefed. They delivered. The copywriter wrote to the brief. It was solid. The strategist handed over a framework. Thorough, structured, professional.

Each part was correct. Nothing connected.

Not because anyone was incompetent. Because there was no center. No one held the thread before anyone else touched the work. The positioning was defined in isolation. The message was written without knowing who'd receive it. The strategy sat in a deck nobody implemented.

Execution without a center is decoration.

We've all been in that room. Everyone nodding at the process, then brief ing without a brief. The work arrived on time. It just didn't arrive together.

The problem wasn't execution. It was upstream of execution.
