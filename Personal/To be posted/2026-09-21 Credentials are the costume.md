Nobody hires an architect because of the university.

They hire them because they can see where the building needs to land — and trust they won't lose that clarity under pressure.

The founders who struggle most with brand foundations make the same mistake. They lead with credentials, tenure, accumulated work.

The client doesn't need your history. They need to know you understand where they're going — and what's currently standing in the way.

Credentials are the costume. Destination is the foundation.
