Everyone has five stars now.

Everyone has "this completely changed how I think about my business."

The testimonial was supposed to be proof. It became a checkbox. And the moment something becomes a checkbox, it stops being proof.

The question isn't which testimonials to trust. It's what replaced them as a real signal.

You watch how someone thinks. Over time. Under pressure. Without a script.

Not what they claim to value. What they do when values cost something.

That's the only thing that can't be fabricated. And the only thing worth trusting.
