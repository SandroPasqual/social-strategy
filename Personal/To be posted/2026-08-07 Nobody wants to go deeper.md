Nobody wants to go deeper.
The founder doesn't. The designer doesn't. One step back and it feels like Mordor — no floor, no light, no way to know if you'll come back with anything useful.
So they start where it feels solid. The logo. The colors. The tone of voice. Things you can see, approve, and ship.
But everything is built on sand.
What's in Mordor? The only thing that makes any of it real.
