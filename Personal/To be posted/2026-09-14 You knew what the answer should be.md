You knew what the answer should be. You asked anyway. The specialist delivered what you wanted to hear.\n\nThat's not a consultation. That's validation disguised as discovery. You paid someone to agree with you so you could move forward without the discomfort of uncertainty.\n\nThe problem isn't that they told you what you wanted. It's that you'll find out later — when the market disagrees and the work doesn't land — that you never actually tested your assumption.\n\nThe most expensive advice is the advice that confirms what you already believe.
