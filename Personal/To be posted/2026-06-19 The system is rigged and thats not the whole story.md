The system is rigged. And that's not the whole story.

Yes, systems are unequal. Networks favor insiders. Luck compounds. Some people start on a tilted board.

That part is true.

But there's a step most people skip.

Between "the system is imperfect" and "my failure is entirely its fault" — there's a question that never gets asked.

What can I control inside a system that isn't fair?

That question is uncomfortable. It requires effort, honesty, and no guarantee of reward. So it disappears.

The absolution asks nothing of you. The question asks everything.

And that's exactly why one spreads faster than the other.
