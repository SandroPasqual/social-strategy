You opened the software before you understood the problem. That's where the drift started.\n\nThe tool gives the illusion of progress. Moving things around feels like thinking. It's not. It's arranging furniture in a room that hasn't been built yet.\n\nThe drift is invisible at first — one decision made without context, then another. Each one reasonable in isolation. Until the gap between what you're building and what you needed becomes too wide to ignore.\n\nBy the time you notice, you're too deep to start over. So you keep going.
