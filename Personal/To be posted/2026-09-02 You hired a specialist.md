It was that the thinking stayed with them. When they left, the logic left with them. What remained was a file you couldn't defend in a conversation.\n\nA system that requires its creator to interpret it isn't a system. It's a dependency with a deadline.\n\nThe question isn't whether they delivered. It's whether what they built can function without them.
