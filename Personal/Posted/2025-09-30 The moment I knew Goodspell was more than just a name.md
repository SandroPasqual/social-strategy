The moment I knew Goodspell was more than just a name

Three months into building my agency, I was still calling it "my atelier."
Clients loved the work — but it felt incomplete. Like performing magic without the incantation.

Then came a late-night struggle with a logo:
What if branding isn't about selling at all?
What if it's about casting the right spell?
Not manipulation. Not tricks.

The kind of spell that makes someone pause and think: this feels different.
That's when Goodspell became inevitable.

The symbol had to follow the same rule: simple, inevitable, precise.
An asterisk — reimagined as a spark.
It works at 16px. It works on a billboard.
But most importantly, it marks that split second when strategy and creativity ignite.

Clients see it differently — a star, a signal, a compass.
For me, it's ignition. The exact moment a brand starts to live.

What started as solving my own naming problem became our philosophy:
Good magic happens when strategy meets emotion, when ideas feel inevitable.

Do you see the spark?
