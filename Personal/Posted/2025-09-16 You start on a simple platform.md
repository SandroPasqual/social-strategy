You start on a "simple" platform. Drag. Drop. Done. First sales roll in. Clients are happy. You feel like you're winning.

Then, slowly, the walls go up:
✅ $29/month… for a font change.
✅ Can't add a field? Wait 18 months for their roadmap.
✅ Want your data back? Good luck exporting that mess.

That's not freedom.
That's rent.

And the worst part?
You built the value — but someone else owns the foundation.

Real freedom means:
Your code.
Your data.
Your timeline.
Your rules.

Yes, it takes more work upfront.
But freedom always does.

So — if you were starting today, would you still pick the same platform?
