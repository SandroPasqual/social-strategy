Learning from the great artists, what drives creativity is often a limited color palette.

Working with a limited palette in branding means setting a small, defined number of colors — not all of them, just a select few — along with a very limited set of fonts and a minimalist logo.

This gives you a solid foundation, the aesthetic vessel you need to create something new every single time.

That's when creativity sparks.
