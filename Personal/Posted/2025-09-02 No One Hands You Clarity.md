No One Hands You Clarity

Buzzwords like 'clarity' don't appear out of nowhere. AI doesn't invent them; it mirrors the patterns we humans already create.

The tricky part? Scroll through feeds, and the word is everywhere. Thousands of posts, all sounding the same. Guilty as charged: I've used it too.

The danger is subtle: when we all echo the same buzzwords, creativity shrinks, original thought hides, and words rarely deliver real clarity anymore.
