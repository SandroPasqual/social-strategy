A logo is not a painting. It's a flag.

Your logo isn't meant to be hung in a gallery. It's meant to be seen everywhere, at any size.

Here's what makes a logo truly powerful:

➤ It's an extraction. It's the simplest visual form of your brand's essence.

➤ It's a symbol. It should work as a single color, at 60px tall, and still be understood.

➤ It's a spark. The best logos have a subtle "aha" moment — a double meaning that creates a memorable connection.

➤ It's functional. Avoid gradients, shadows, and excessive detail. They cause endless reproduction issues and fail at small sizes.

Complexity is easy. Simplicity is genius.

A simple logo isn't empty. It's focused.
