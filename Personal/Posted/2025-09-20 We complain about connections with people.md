We complain about "connections" with people we don't know.
We accept "friends" we've never tested.

Real relationships are slow.
They're earned through struggle, presence, and showing up again and again.

We invented consistency — just like we invented democracy — to live safer, to live better.
We can reinvent connection — to live more truthfully.

And yet, here on LinkedIn — among founders, builders, and fellow designers —

I've met good people.
