In ancient Rome, painting, design, and drawing were NOT considered art.

The frescoes of Pompeii? Just decoration.
Sculpture? Still a craft.

Only in the Renaissance, when emotions came alive on canvas and stone, did visual arts claim their spot as true art.

Today, is what we do art or craft? If it's just skill, it's craft. If it stirs the soul, it's art. What's your take?
