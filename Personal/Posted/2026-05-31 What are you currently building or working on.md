What are you currently building or working on?
A strategic practice — Goodspell — focused on one problem: the gap between what a founder has built and how it reads publicly. The work is positioning and perception, not design.

What's one challenge or opportunity you're focused on right now?
Founders using AI are moving faster than ever — shipping products, scaling operations, building real things. But the public narrative around what they're building rarely keeps up. The execution accelerates. The positioning stays vague or generic. That gap has a cost.

What excites you most about AI agents or autonomous systems?
The speed compression. What used to take months now takes weeks. That makes the clarity of your positioning more important, not less — because more gets built, faster, with less time to course-correct on how it's understood externally.
