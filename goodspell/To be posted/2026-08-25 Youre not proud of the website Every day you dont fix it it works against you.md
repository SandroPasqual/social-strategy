You're not proud of the website.

You know it. Every time you share it, you know it. Every time someone searches your name and lands there, you know it. It's not catastrophically bad — it's just not right. Not wrong enough to justify stopping everything to fix it. Not right enough to feel confident sending it.

So it stays.

Every day it stays, it works. Just not for you.

It works for the prospect forming an impression before the first call. It works for the candidate evaluating the offer. It works for the partner deciding whether to make the introduction. It works for the competitor who looks more serious because their presence is current and yours isn't.

The website you're not proud of is the one doing the work right now. The one making the case while you're busy doing everything else.

It doesn't wait for you to be ready to fix it.
