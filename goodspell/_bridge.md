## Relationship with goodspell.online

**Path:** `../../goodspell.online/`

**Role:** social-strategy generates recognition. goodspell.online confirms it.

**How social feeds the site:**
- The site is a source of authority. Posts can reference it for depth.
- Concepts from Framework appear on the site as situations, not technical terms.

**How the site feeds social:**
- The tone, voice, and structure of the site mirror what works in posts.
- Posts that generate strong "that's me" reactions become anchor points on the site.

**Rule:** Any post that generates enough interest for someone to look up the site must have a counterpart on the site that confirms what they felt.

## Relationship with the portal

**Path:** `../../goodspell.online/portal/`

**Role:** social-strategy is the magnet. The portal is where people land.

**How social feeds the portal:**
- Posts prepare the ground. When the client reaches the portal, the concepts are already familiar.

**How the portal feeds social:**
- Every engagement produces insights that (anonymized) become posts.

**Rule:** Nothing from the portal enters social without anonymization.
