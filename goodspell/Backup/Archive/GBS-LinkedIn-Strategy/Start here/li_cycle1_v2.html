Framework Post 02 · Week 1 · Thursday
Zero Wasting Rebranding
Four decisions
before you change
anything.
01 Document the positioning. What the business is, who it is for, why it is the right choice.
02 Identify what is actually broken. Visual, structural, or strategic.
03 Define what must not change. Brand equity is invisible until it is destroyed.
04 Build a system, not a deliverable. It must work without the founder in the room.
