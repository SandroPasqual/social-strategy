## Post Body
There's a client you haven't approached.

You know who they are. You've thought about it more than once. The work is exactly right for them — the fit is obvious, the timing is good, the conversation would be worth having.

But you haven't started it.

Not because you're not ready. Not because the offer isn't solid. Because before you reach out, they will look you up. And what they find needs to match what you're about to say.

Right now it doesn't.

So the client stays on the list. The conversation stays hypothetical. The opportunity sits in a decision you're not quite ready to make — not because the work isn't there, but because the presence isn't ready to be scrutinized by exactly the kind of client you most want to work with.

The clients you hesitate to approach tell you exactly where the presence is failing.
