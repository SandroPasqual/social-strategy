## Post Body
You left with a track record that should have closed any room.

Years of serious work. Results that mattered. A network that knew exactly what you were capable of. The kind of history that, properly represented, removes the need to convince anyone of anything.

You've been carrying it yourself ever since.

In every introduction, every pitch, every conversation where you start from the beginning because the presence hasn't found a way to carry what you built before. The track record is real. It just lives in you — in your words, your memory, your ability to describe it to each new person who needs to understand it.

A presence that carries weight does the work before you speak. It translates the track record into something a stranger can evaluate without needing you to explain it.

You earned the right to walk into any room with authority. The presence should be doing that for you.
