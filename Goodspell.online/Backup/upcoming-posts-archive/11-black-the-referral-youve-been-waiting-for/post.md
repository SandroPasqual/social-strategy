## Post Body
You have a referral sitting in your inbox. The kind you've been waiting for.

The right industry. The right size. The kind of client that changes the trajectory of the business. Someone you respect made the introduction. The email is warm. The timing is good.

And you haven't replied yet.

Not because you're not interested. Not because you're too busy. Because the moment you reply, they will look you up. And the version of you they find needs to match the introduction they just received.

You're not sure it does.

So the email sits. A few hours become a day. You tell yourself you're preparing a thoughtful response. What you're actually doing is buying time for a presence that isn't ready.

The referral didn't fail. The introduction didn't fail. You haven't failed.

The presence failed — before the conversation started.
