# Posting Rhythm

## Question
What rhythm can be sustained given current constraints?

## Constraints
- Solo founder, no dedicated publisher
- Buffer free: 10 post max
- Images not yet produced at scale
- Quality must not drop below The Recipe standard

## Options
1. **2x/week rigid** — original plan. Requires dedicated time for images + scheduling. Not sustainable currently.
2. **Batch + drip** — prepare 4–6 posts in a batch. Schedule what fits in Buffer. Post when slots free up. Irregular but consistent in quality.
3. **Text-first, images-later** — post text-only on a loose rhythm. Produce images separately and add to existing posts or future cycle.
