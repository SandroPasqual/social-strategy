# Positioning Statement

Source: `../GBS Goodspell/0108 Strategic Positioning Statement _ Goodspell _ OUTPUT.md`

## Market Category
High-Precision Brand Strategy Consultancy. Not a design studio. Not a creative agency. A firm that builds documented, governable brand systems — operational infrastructure that functions independently of the founder's daily presence.

## Primary Audience
The Professional in Transition — experienced professionals at a deliberate inflection point. Launching an independent practice, exiting a firm, or repositioning an established business.

## Secondary Audience
The Established Operator — founders of serious businesses (5–15 years, $300K–$5M revenue) whose brand has not kept pace with the business. The Maturity Gap is present and producing friction.

## The Problem
The brand strategy market produces deliverables designed to be presented, approved, and filed. The result: the deliverable depreciates. Within 18–24 months, the brand is drifting again.

## The Unlock
The shift from brand as deliverable to brand as system. A system is documented, numbered, and addressable. It governs decisions. It can be applied by anyone who has access to it. Its value compounds over time.

## Core Message
Goodspell builds brand systems for serious founders and professionals in transition — not deliverables to approve, but infrastructure to operate.
