# Schedule — Plan vs Reality

## Current Reality (as of April 2026)
- 9 posts published, text-only, without images
- Irregular intervals: 37m, 2d, 6d, 1w, 1w, 1w...
- 2 followers
- Buffer free plan: max 10 scheduled posts
- No dedicated person to handle publishing — posts when founder can

## Original Plan
- Tuesday + Thursday, 2 posts/week
- 22 weeks, 44 posts total
- Each post: image (520×520) + text (150–180 words)
- WHITE/BLACK alternation

## The Gap
Calendar was designed for consistent 2x/week with visuals.
Reality is text-only, irregular, constrained by available time and Buffer limits.

## Decision Needed
- Decouple text from images: text goes first, images added later
- Work in batches of 3–4 prepared posts
- Maintain quality (The Recipe) but drop the rigid schedule
- Document actual publishing dates here, not planned ones
