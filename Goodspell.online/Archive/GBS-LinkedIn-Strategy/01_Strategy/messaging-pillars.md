# Messaging Pillars

Source: `../GBS Goodspell/0202 Messaging Framework _ Goodspell _ OUTPUT.md`

Every LinkedIn post must advance at least one of these five pillars.

## Pillar 1 — The methodology is the credential
Goodspell's authority comes from a documented, structured, three-phase methodology built on 25 years at the intersection of strategy and visual execution. The system exists before any engagement begins — and the buyer can see it before committing.

## Pillar 2 — The output is a system, not a deliverable
What Goodspell produces is not a brand book designed for approval — it is a documented, governable brand system the client can operate independently. A deliverable depreciates from the moment it is filed. A system compounds in value every time it is used correctly.

## Pillar 3 — The engagement respects the founder's time
Async-first, milestone-based, portal-managed. The founder is consulted at critical decisions — not asked to manage the process. The system does the heavy lifting. The client does the thinking.

## Pillar 4 — The price is fixed because the value is known
Tier 2 — Brand Foundation at $6,000. Tier 3 — Strategic Authority at $12,000. Published. Not subject to negotiation. Fixed pricing is the first operational signal of strategic confidence.

## Pillar 5 — The design background is the strategic credential
25 years of executing brand decisions at the visual level is not a prior career — it is the evidence base for the strategic methodology. A strategist who has never designed anything is theorizing. A strategist who has spent 25 years at the point where strategy meets execution has earned the conclusions.
