# Hashtag Strategy

## Current Hashtags
All 9 published posts use the same three:
- #BrandStrategy
- #Positioning
- #BusinessGrowth

## Strategy
Consistency over variety. The same three hashtags on every post.

### Why
- Builds association between Goodspell and these three terms
- No evidence that rotating hashtags improves reach on LinkedIn Company Pages
- Keeps the decision simple: no hashtag selection per post
- All three are category-relevant without being oversaturated

### The Three
| Hashtag | Role |
|---|---|
| #BrandStrategy | Primary — what Goodspell does |
| #Positioning | Secondary — the specific discipline |
| #BusinessGrowth | Tertiary — the outcome the buyer cares about |

### Rules
- Always these three, in this order
- After the last line of the post, never in the body
- No additional hashtags
- No campaign-specific or trending hashtags

## Review
Revisit after 50 posts or if LinkedIn changes hashtag behavior significantly.
